Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies only to Cellular-Pro products and services. Last updated: 27 September 2024

We understand the importance of your personal information and are committed to protecting it securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information. Please read this policy carefully before using our application.

1. Information We Collect and Use

With your permission, the app may collect the following information:

  • GPS location (used for map display and route tracking)
  • IP address
  • Android ID
  • Device model and system version
  • Country and mobile network information (MCC/MNC)

This information is used only to provide application functionality, improve performance, and support membership services.

2. Permissions Used

  • Storage permission
    Used to save configuration files, signaling logs, exported test data, and application settings.
  • Location permission
    Used to display device location and collect cellular network parameters such as eNodeB ID, EARFCN, and PCI.
  • Phone state permission
    Used to obtain cellular signal strength and support network locking features.
  • Foreground service permission
    Used to ensure stability during long-running test operations such as drive testing and automated scripts.

3. Root (su) Access

The app may request root (su) access only with your explicit permission and only on devices that have already been rooted by the user.

Root access is used exclusively for advanced cellular engineering features, including:

  • Network locking (NR, LTE, WCDMA, GSM)
  • Frequency band locking
  • EARFCN and PCI locking
  • Collecting chipset signaling data
  • Displaying advanced cellular parameters

The app does not use root access for any unrelated purposes.

4. Logging and Diagnostics

The app may generate log files for troubleshooting and diagnostic purposes. These logs do not contain sensitive personal information such as phone numbers, addresses, or private content.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. Sensitive data is transmitted using secure encryption methods.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required to provide application functionality.
