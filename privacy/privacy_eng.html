     1. How do we collect and use your personal information
We will explain the types of personal information we collect from your device and their corresponding purposes one by one, so that you can understand the types of personal information we collect for a specific function, the reasons for its use and the way it is collected.
      1.1 we will try to collect some sensitive information from your device after obtaining your permission, which includes: GPS information, IP Address,Android ID, Device model etc, The GPS information is used to display the current location in app. The APP also uses Andoid ID to tag different users.and the same time if you has been registered to be member, app will get your Country name, MCC(Mobile Country Code),Phone model,Order information and Android ID .and these information will be saved in server and to be supported for your member service.
 
app will try to apply for the following permissions:
      ● Storage permission (read/write storage) : if the android version is Android 10 or lower, App will apply Storage permission  to store the necessary application configuration files and task test data files, signaling  export, test track export, etc., your storage permission needs to be invoked.
      ● Location permission(fine and coarse location)  : Cellular parameters  collection( such Enodeb ID, Earfcn,PCI etc) .outdoor map page displays real-time track.       
      ● Read Phone State:  Locking RAT(support NR,LTE,WCDMA,GSM), and Collect celluar signal strength. such as LTE RSRP etc.
      ● Foreground service permission: In order to support test script can run by scedule. such as Long-call, DT test etc. apply this permission incease App priority.
 
     In addition, the app will use a logging function to record its behavior. If necessary, you can provide us with log information to assist in problem analyzation. The log will not record your sensitive information, such as address, phone number, etc.
     After obtaining your permission, the app will attempt to apply su privileges, It's mainly for frequency locking, rat locking, cell locking, collect signaling and getting more cellular parameters. The  prerequisite for execution is that your device has already been rooted and the chipiset is Qualcomm and MTK.
   The purpose of obtaining su permission for the App
     ● Collect raw chipset data, decode it and provide it to the interface for use, including parameters and signaling output.
     ● Implement network and frequency locking operations. Including network locking, band , locking arfcn, and pci.
     ● Implement make phone call and hangup when executing Outgoing call and Incoming call.
  In addition, the APP does not perform any operations unrelated to its functions.
 
 [Remark]
     ● Due to the need to render the outdoor dot track in real time, the frequency of GPS acquisition is 1-2 seconds.
     ● The frequency of the base station is collected in 1-2 seconds as it needs to render the trend diagram of cell level in real time.
     ●  Android needs to access GPS to obtain cell information. Disabling GPS will result in failure to display network signal strength.
     ●  Disabling the permission to Read Phone State will cause the  Locking RAT can not work properly.
