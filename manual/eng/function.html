3 Signaling view

Signaling is the core content of the interaction between mobile phone and base station. All kinds of measurement information reporting and measurement control are reflected through signaling. In terms of direction,signaling is divided into two directions: uplink (from mobile phone to base station) and downlink (from base station to mobile phone).Signaling is layered. Taking LTE as an example, it can be divided into RRC,ESM,EMM and SIP.RRC signaling is reclassified by logical channel. At present, APP only supports  layer 3 messages.
User can click one item to see the detailed decoder information.
signaling filter. And you can select special signaling group to display
